About us:

The Adeptus Mechanicus is the official Imperial name within the Adeptus Terra for the Cult Mechanicus or Cult of the Machine based on Mars which provides the Imperium with its scientists, engineers and technicians.

Our privileges

    1. An immortal mech-body that does not know disease and fatigue.
    2. Extensive knowledge library.
    3. Promotion on a career ladder.
    4. Close-knit team working for the common good.

Our requirements

    1. Bless the Emperor of Mankind.
    2. Do not be registered with the Inquisition.
    3. Hate Xenos!

How to enter?

    1. Apply at the nearest qualifying point or fill the form on this page.
    2. Be ready to rebuild.
    3. Pray the Emperor of Mankind for success.
